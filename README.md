## üéØ About the Certification
The AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner validates foundational, high-level knowledge of AWS Cloud services and general cloud computing concepts. This certification is ideal for individuals in technical, managerial, sales, purchasing, or financial roles who work with the AWS Cloud.
Exam Domains Covered:

Cloud Concepts (24%) - Understanding of cloud computing principles and AWS Cloud value proposition
Security and Compliance (30%) - AWS shared responsibility model, security best practices, and compliance concepts
Cloud Technology and Services (34%) - Core AWS services across compute, storage, database, networking, and more
Billing, Pricing, and Support (12%) - AWS pricing models, account structures, billing tools, and support options

Exam Details:
Score: 700/1000

## üí° My Study Approach
Timeline

Total Study Duration: 2 Weeks of focus study
Daily Commitment: 2-3 hours per day on weekdays, 4-5 hours on weekends, 8 hours a day before the exam

Study Strategy

Video Course Completion: Watched the entire 14-hour freeCodeCamp course, taking detailed notes
Hands-On Practice: Created a free AWS account and practiced with services in the Free Tier
Note Organization: Organized notes by exam domain for easy review
Active Recall: Used flashcards and self-quizzing to reinforce concepts
Practice Exams: Took multiple practice tests to identify weak areas
Review and Revision: reviewed all notes and retaking weak sections

What Worked Best

Breaking down the 14-hour course into manageable 1-2 hour study sessions
Immediately practicing concepts in the AWS console after learning them
Creating comparison charts for similar services (e.g., S3 vs. EBS vs. EFS)
Understanding the "why" behind AWS services, not just memorizing features
Focusing on use cases and scenarios rather than just technical specifications

## üìà Exam Experience
Preparation Tips

Understand the AWS Shared Responsibility Model thoroughly
Know when to use each service and their primary use cases
Focus on high-level concepts rather than deep technical details
Understand AWS pricing models and cost optimization strategies
Be familiar with AWS support plans and their features

Exam Day Strategy

Don't let your computer crash in the middle of the exam like me üòî
Read each question carefully and identify key requirements
Use process of elimination for difficult questions
Flag uncertain questions for review
Manage time effectively (90 minutes for 65 questions)
Stay calm and trust your preparation
